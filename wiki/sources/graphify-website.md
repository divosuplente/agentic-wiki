## Summary
Graphify is an open-source platform that builds a queryable knowledge graph from multi-modal codebases—code, documentation, papers, and diagrams—by combining Tree-sitter static analysis with LLM-driven semantic extraction. It enables AI coding assistants to understand not just what code does, but why it was designed that way, achieving dramatic token-efficiency improvements over naive approaches.
