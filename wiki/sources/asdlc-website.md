## Summary
ASDLC.io documents the principles, patterns, and standards for transitioning software development from craft-based to industrial agent-driven models. It defines three pillars — Factory Architecture, Standardized Parts, and Quality Control — and provides an MCP endpoint, downloadable static skill, and schema-validated content for agent-driven development workflows.
