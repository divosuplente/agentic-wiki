## Description
An AI-native software development platform that puts **documentation, collaboration, and oversight** at the center of software development. Built for regulated enterprises — healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, federal government.

> "8090 is the AI-native software development platform that keeps business leaders in the driver's seat. From business intent to enterprise-grade software with quality, control, and consistency at every step." — Chamath Palihapitiya, Co-founder and CEO, 8090

> "Leveraging 8090's platform, we are moving beyond just code and prototypes to deliver complete, commercial-grade products at pace." — Colm Sparks-Austin, EY Americas Technology Consulting Leader

## Two Products

### Software Factory
The AI-native SDLC control plane. Brings your team and AI agents into a single system to build software together. Define intent, coordinate execution, and maintain full control, visibility, and auditability over every decision.

### 8090 Enterprise
Purpose-built applications designed around your workflows. 8090 designs, builds, hosts, and maintains. You own the business logic and stay in control.

## Key Principles
- **Quality. Control. Consistency.** — at every step
- **Business leaders define what gets built in plain English** before a single line of code
- **Tribal knowledge dies. Documentation lives.** — institutional knowledge becomes a living knowledge graph
