## Summary
Microsoft's guided technical labs provide hands-on experience with Azure, AI services, Copilot integrations, and cloud-native development. Labs are free, browser-based, and self-paced.

## Coverage
- Azure OpenAI — deploying and fine-tuning models
- Copilot extensibility — building Copilot plugins and skills
- ML ops — MLOps pipelines and monitoring
- Cloud-native development — containers, serverless, and DevOps

## Key Takeaways
- Covers Azure OpenAI, Copilot extensibility, and ML ops.
- Interactive sandboxes eliminate local setup friction.
- Credits and achievements integrate with Microsoft Learn profiles.
- Self-paced with progress tracking.

## Recommended Audience
.NET/Azure developers and enterprise teams adopting Microsoft AI tooling.
