## Summary
Google Developers breaks down the T-shaped engineer model: deep expertise in one domain plus broad knowledge across adjacent areas. In the AI era, this shape is evolving to include AI tooling literacy as part of the horizontal bar.

## The T-Shaped Model
- **Vertical bar** — deep expertise in one domain (e.g., distributed systems, frontend, ML)
- **Horizontal bar** — broad knowledge across adjacent areas (architecture, testing, DevOps, UX)
- **In the AI era** — the horizontal bar now includes prompt engineering, model evaluation, and agent orchestration

## Key Takeaways
- Depth still matters; agents amplify experts more than generalists.
- Breadth now includes prompt engineering, model evaluation, and agent orchestration.
- Teams composed of complementary T-shaped profiles ship faster.
- AI tooling literacy is the new baseline for the horizontal bar.

## Recommended Audience
Early-career engineers and mentors designing growth paths.
