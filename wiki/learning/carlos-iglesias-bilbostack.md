## Summary
Carlos Iglesias presents the #NoProduct framework at BilboStack, arguing for building tools that solve real problems without the overhead of traditional product management. The talk is a call for impact-driven, AI-accelerated development.

## Core Concept: #NoProduct
- **Skip product management overhead** — roadmaps, backlog grooming, and feature prioritization can slow down AI-assisted teams
- **Impact-driven growth** — prioritise measurable outcomes over formal roadmaps
- **Build real tools** — focus on solving problems, not shipping products
- **Especially suited for** internal tools, rapid experiments, and proof-of-concepts

## Key Takeaways
- Product management overhead can slow down AI-assisted teams.
- Impact-driven growth prioritises measurable outcomes over roadmaps.
- NoProduct is especially suited for internal tools and rapid experiments.
- AI acceleration makes the "build first, plan later" approach viable for more use cases.

## Recommended Audience
Tech leads, founders, and developers building lean internal products.
