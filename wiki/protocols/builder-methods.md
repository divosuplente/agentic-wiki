## Description
Training, community, and resources for building with AI. For business owners and operators building real tools with AI. Development experience optional.

> "Stay ahead. Skip the hype. Become the builder."

## By
Brian Casel — 20 years building software as a developer, designer, and founder of multiple SaaS products.

## Philosophy
"Demos make it look easier than it is. Most people get a prototype working, then stall out. A prototype isn't a real product — the craft is what closes that gap."

## Offerings

### Training
Video training courses that go beyond "vibe coding" to show you how to build for real. Essential concepts, proven workflows, and the architecture and process behind tools that actually hold up in production.

### Starter Kits
Ready-to-build starter kits — specs, PRDs, designs, and prompts — for the apps, agents, and internal tools you'll actually use. Clone, customize, ship.

### Community
An active community of hundreds of builders shipping their own tools with AI. Live sessions, Q&A, show and tell, and pro builders on hand when you need a second set of eyes.

### Content
- **YouTube** — latest workflows and concepts, new videos weekly

- **Articles** — essays on methods, mindset, and the shift in software
