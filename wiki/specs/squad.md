## Description
Repository for multi-agent orchestration and coding workflow automation by Brady Gaster. Provides patterns and utilities for forming teams of AI agents (squads) that can work together on complex development tasks.

## Philosophy
Squad treats agents as team members with distinct roles — architect, implementer, tester, reviewer — that collaborate under a structured workflow. Emphasizes role specialization and handoff patterns between agents.
