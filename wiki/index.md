## Summary
This wiki is a structured, interlinked knowledge base about AI coding agents, skills, harnesses, and standards. It follows the Karpathy pattern of raw sources, schema, and AI-maintained pages to keep institutional knowledge discoverable and up to date.

## Execution Surfaces
Agents, terminals, IDEs, and environments where AI-assisted development actually happens — the places where natural language gets turned into shipped code.

→ [Execution Surfaces Index](execution-surfaces/index.md)

## Governance

→ [Governance Index](governance/index.md)

## Protocols
Interfaces, communication standards, and integration mechanisms that allow AI agents, tools, and services to interoperate — including MCP servers, knowledge graphs, and documentation delivery systems.

→ [Protocols Index](protocols/index.md)

## Ecosystem

→ [Ecosystem Index](ecosystem/index.md)

## Specs

→ [Specs Index](specs/index.md)
